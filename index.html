Frequently Asked Questions

What services are included in gutter repair in London?

Gutter repair services in London typically include fixing leaks, replacing damaged gutter sections or clips, sealing seams and joints, correcting sagging runs, and ensuring proper drainage — often alongside gutter cleaning and inspection.

How often should I get my gutters cleaned in London?

Gutters should generally be cleaned at least once a year, but if your property is near trees or exposed to heavy weather you may need cleaning twice a year to avoid blockages and water damage.

How do you seal gutter seams to stop leaks?

To seal gutter seams, first clean and dry the area, then apply a weatherproof gutter sealant or specialist tape specifically designed for gutters over the seam or joint to create a watertight seal.

What’s the best way to seal gutter leaks?

The most effective way to seal gutter leaks is to locate the leak, clean the surface thoroughly, and use a flexible, waterproof gutter sealant applied over the crack or joint; larger holes may need a patch and sealant.

Can gutter leaks be fixed with just cleaning?

Cleaning alone may help if a blockage is causing overflow, but actual leaks at seams or cracks require sealing or repair of the affected parts to stop water escaping through damaged areas.
